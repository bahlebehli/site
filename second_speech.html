A speech about drug abuse


Greetings l Bahle Ndhlovu would like to tell you all a speech about the causes, effects, symptoms, and preventions of drug abuse. l am sharing this speech with you all so that you can know how to save others from it by knowing how dangerous it can be to them.

Drug abuse is the use of a drug in amounts or methods which can be harmful to individuals or others. lt is also known as substance abuse. There are different types of drugs that can be abused by people all over the world like alcohol, cocaine, and marijuana. The causes for people to use these drugs are boredom, loss of employment, and increased stressors such as illness. The uses of these drugs can sometimes lead to effects such as mental confusion, stroke, various forms of cancer, and lung diseases.

There are also symptoms of drug abuse like mood swings, not maintaining personal hygiene, and feeling depressed all the time. ln addition of all that l have said we also have the preventions of drug abuse like strengthening of personal commitments against drug abuse, being a good role model, and studying habits and academic support.

As a nation we are stronger and we can fight against drug abuse.

Thank you all for taking your time to read this speech
